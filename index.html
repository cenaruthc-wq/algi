Algebraic Expression

Expression in mathematics that combine variables, constant and arithmetic operations to show relations between quantities.

Polynomial

Expression of terms with constants and variables raised to non-negative exponents.

Adding Polynomials

To add polynomials simply combine the like terms or similar terms. To combine similar terms, get the sum of numerical coefficient and copy the same numerical coefficient then simplify.

Example: (5z + 12)+(3z+2) = (5z+3z=8z)+ (12+2=14) = 8z+14

Subtracting Polynomials

To subtract a polynomial to another polynomial, change the sign of the subtrahend and proceed to addition, after combine the like terms then simplify

Example: 5z - 12z = 5z + (-12z) = -7z

(2x+3)+(4x+1)= 6x+4
(5x-2)-(3x+1)= 2x-3
(3x²+2x)+(x²-x)=4x²+x
